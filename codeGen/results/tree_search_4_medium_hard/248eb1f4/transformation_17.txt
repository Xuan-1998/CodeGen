dp[i][j] represents the index of the winner when there are i players, and j people have coins in their hands.
dp[0][0] = 1 (base case)
for i from 1 to X:
    for j from 0 to M-1:
        if j == M-1: dp[i][j] = (j+1)%i (since the last person gets eliminated after M-1 rounds)
        else:
            winner = (dp[i-1][(j+M-2)%i]+1)%i
            dp[i][j] = winner
return dp[X][0]
